FAQ

What is a Solana PnL tracker?

A Solana PnL tracker helps users review positions, realized profit and loss, receipts, and trade history across wallet and bot workflows.

Why do Telegram traders need PnL receipts?

Receipts make calls and results easier to review, share, and compare after a fast-moving token leaves the chat feed.

Is PnL always instant on-chain?

No. PnL depends on wallet data, token prices, liquidity, and refresh timing, so good tools should show status and avoid pretending stale data is fresh.
