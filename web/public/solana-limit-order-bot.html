FAQ

What is a Solana limit order bot?

A Solana limit order bot helps users plan triggered buy or sell actions based on configured market conditions.

Do limit orders always execute exactly?

No. Execution depends on liquidity, route availability, slippage, wallet state, and market movement.

Why do limit orders need receipts?

Receipts help users confirm what triggered, when it happened, and what token or wallet was involved.
