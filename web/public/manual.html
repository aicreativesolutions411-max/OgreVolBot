How To Trade

Two ways to buy: Quick Buy (fastest) and Slime Swap (full control). Here's the whole flow.

⚡ Quick Buy — the fast lane

Find a coin — tap one in any feed, or paste its contract address (CA) in the search.
Tap Quick Buy. A little panel opens.
Type how much SOL to spend (e.g. 0.10) — or tap a size preset.
Hit Buy & approve. Your fill lands in Positions. Done.

🟢 Slime Swap — full control

Open Slime Swap (or tap a coin → it loads there).
Paste the token CA (or it's already filled from the coin you tapped).
Pick the wallet to trade from.
Set your amount and slippage (see presets below).
Tap Buy → approve. To sell later, flip the route or use the sell % presets.

💸 Selling & exiting

Go to Positions — every open bag is listed with live PnL.
Tap the position and choose a sell %: 25% 50% 100% or custom — great for taking partial profit.
Or set an auto-exit plan (Take Profit / Stop Loss) so it sells for you — see presets.
Golden rule: memecoins are high-risk. Only spend what you can afford to lose, and check the SlimeShield read before you ape.

Quick-Trade Presets

Presets are one-tap settings so you trade fast without typing every time. Set your defaults in Home / Profile; tweak per-trade in the panel. Here's what each one means:

💰 Buy Size 0.1 SOL 0.25 0.5 custom

How much SOL a one-tap buy spends. Tap a preset instead of typing the amount every time.

🌀 Slippage 3% 4% 5% custom bps

How much price movement you'll accept on the fill. Fresh/volatile coins need higher slippage to go through. Custom is in bps (basis points): 100 bps = 1%, so 500 = 5%.

📤 Sell % 25% 50% 100% custom

How much of a bag to dump in one tap. Use 25–50% to take profit and ride the rest.

🎯 Take Profit +25% +60% +100% +250%

Auto-sell when it pumps to your target. Custom accepts a % like 500 or a multiple like 5x.

🛑 Stop Loss off -8% -10% -15%

Auto-sell to cut a loser. Set it when you open a position so you don't round-trip a rug.

⏱️ Fallback Sell timer

Auto-sells after a set time if neither TP nor SL hits — so nothing sits forgotten.

⚙️ Priority Fee

Pay a little extra for faster confirmation when the chain is busy / on snipes.

Newbie defaults that just work: Buy 0.1 SOL · Slippage 4% · Take Profit +60% · Stop Loss -10%. Adjust as you get a feel for it.

Pro Tools

Inside Ogre Tek you get the heavy machinery. Same presets apply (size, slippage, TP/SL).

📦 Bundle

Buy or sell the same token across several wallets from one panel — paste the CA, pick wallets, set buy-per-wallet + sell %, go. Add an optional Auto-Exit plan (TP/SL + fallback timer) for all of them.

🎯 Snipe

Set up a buy that fires the instant a target launches or hits your condition — size + slippage + priority fee preset, then it executes for you.

🤖 Ogre A.I. / SlimeBot

Hands-off automation that hunts and trades to your rules (size, TP, SL, limits). Start small, set hard limits, watch it in Positions.

All automation respects your presets and limits. You can stop any of it from its panel at any time.

The Swamp Game

Open slimewire.org/swamp or tap Play The Swamp. Free, in your browser, built on real live coins.

🎮 Move & explore

Drag the left side of the screen to move (mobile) or use WASD / arrows (desktop). Pick your ogre on the title screen. Live coins roam as wild creatures — fresh launches are rare, SlimeWire launches are legendary.

✋ Catch coins (build your Dex)

Walk up to a creature and tap ✋ Catch — you'll see its real market cap, age and momentum.
Weaken it first: tap ⚔️ Weaken to drop its HP. Lower HP = much higher catch chance.
Time the bar and tap 🟢 Throw. Caught coins go in your Swampdex with a rarity.
Slimeballs are catch energy — they refill over time, or craft more from resources.

⚔️ Fight for resources

Hostile mobs (goblins, wolves, bears) roam too. Tap the ⚔️ Attack button to fight them; they drop wood, scrap & goo. Spend those at the Clan Camp to craft slimeballs and fortify your base.

🗓️ Raid days, clans & survival

  • Raid Day (Sat 2pm → Sun 2am UTC): more & tougher enemies. Rest of week = build & gather. Watch the HUD countdown.
  • Clans — set a tag at the Clan Camp; everyone with your tag is a crew competing together.
  • Other traders walk the same map live.
Free forever. Trading just rewards you: your real Swamp Worth buffs your run, and launching a coin earns a legendary. You never have to trade to play.

Tips & FAQ

Do I need a wallet to play?

No. The game, raids and all pages are free with no login. A wallet is only for buying, selling or launching.

What slippage should I use?

Start at 4%. Bump to 5–10% on brand-new/volatile coins if buys fail. Remember custom is in bps (500 = 5%).

How do I take profit automatically?

Set a Take Profit preset (e.g. +60% or custom 5x) and a Stop Loss (e.g. -10%) when you open the trade.

How do I get my group competing?

Pick a clan tag, have everyone set it in the game + on /raids, and climb the /crews board.

The full loop: scan /radar → ape or raid a coin → catch it in the /swamp → post your /receipts card → bring your crew on /crews. Repeat. 🐸🔥
