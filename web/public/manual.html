Get A Trading Wallet

Before you can buy, you need a wallet with SOL in it. Open the Wallet tab — you've got two ways in.

🔗 Connect & fund (recommended)

Tap Connect and pick Phantom or Solflare. Approve in your wallet popup.
SlimeWire offers to spin up a fresh trading wallet for you and asks how much SOL to move into it.
You approve that transfer in your own wallet — SlimeWire never holds your keys. The new wallet is funded and ready.
A backup file + recovery keys are generated automatically. Save them somewhere safe.

✨ Create an empty wallet

Prefer to fund it later? Tap Create Wallet to make a fresh trading wallet with a zero balance, then send SOL to its address whenever you're ready.

Save your backup. Your recovery file is the only way back into a trading wallet if you lose your device. No backup = no recovery. Store it offline.
Why a trading wallet? It keeps your main wallet clean — you fund the trading wallet with only what you want to play with, and the terminal buys and sells from it instantly.

Find Coins — The Tabs

The Trending screen is your hunting ground. Each tab pulls a different slice of the market, so you can find exactly the kind of coin you're after.

🔥 Trending

The hottest pairs right now by real momentum and volume.

🌱 New

Brand-new launches, streaming in seconds after they go live.

📈 Surging

Coins jumping hard on the short timeframe — movers with green candles.

💧 Top Volume

Where the real money is flowing — highest 24h trade volume.

🎓 Graduating

Pump.fun coins close to bonding (~$69K cap) — about to hit the open market.

🪙 Migrated

Coins that already graduated and moved to a full DEX pool.

Reading a row

Every row shows the coin's price, market cap, age, volume and price move at a glance. Where a coin has them, its social links (X, Telegram, website) sit right next to the copy-address button — tap to check the project before you ape.

Tip: start in Trending or Surging to see what's moving, use Graduating to catch pump coins right before they bond, and New if you want to be earliest. Paste any contract address (CA) in search to jump straight to a coin.

Watchlist — Save Your Coins

Spotted something but not ready to buy? Star it. Your Watchlist keeps every saved coin in one place with live prices.

Tap the ☆ star on any coin — on a Trending row, a Trenches card, or the coin page. It turns ★ gold.
Open your saved coins any time from the ★ star button in the top bar (next to your wallet), or the ★ Watchlist tab on the Trending screen.
Prices and market caps stay live, so your list is always current. Tap a coin to trade it; tap ★ again to remove it.
Always one tap away. The gold star in the top bar lights up whenever you're viewing your Watchlist — your personal shortlist of coins to keep an eye on.

Buy & Sell — One Tap

The fastest way to trade. Set your presets once, then every buy is a single tap.

⚡ First time you tap Buy

Tap Buy on any coin row. The first time, a quick-buy menu opens.
Set your presets: how much SOL to spend, plus an optional take-profit and stop-loss.
Save them. From now on, tapping Buy fires instantly at your preset — no menu, no confirm dialog.
Built for speed. Once your presets are set, the bar amount or a preset chip buys on the first tap. You can change presets any time in the Wallet → Presets tab.

💸 Selling

Open the coin's Positions tab (or the Portfolio page) to see the bag with live PnL.
Pick a sell % — 25% 50% 100% or custom — and it sells from that wallet.
Or rely on the take-profit / stop-loss you set at buy time to exit automatically.

Launch Sniper — Be First

Don't want to be late to a coin you're waiting for? The Launch Sniper (in the Tools menu) watches the live launch stream and auto-buys the instant your coin appears — server-side, so it fires even with the app closed.

Open Launch Sniper. A fast feed of brand-new launches scrolls in real time — tap ⚡ Buy on any to ape it now.
To snipe one that hasn't launched yet, type the ticker or name and pick your buy preset, then Arm sniper.
The moment a matching coin is created, SlimeWire buys it instantly on the server (sub-second), then disarms so it only fires once.
You get a 🎯 push notification the second it snipes, and the bag shows up in Portfolio.
Turn on alerts (Wallet → Enable alerts) so the snipe ping reaches your phone. The sniper keeps watching on the server even if you close the tab.
Real buy: an armed sniper spends real SOL the instant it fires. Only arm it with a preset size you're comfortable buying blind.

Swap

One panel for any trade. Swap lives in its own tab — no jumping around.

Open Swap. Pick from the popular pairs or paste a custom CA.
Set your amount and slippage.
Tap the flip button to switch between buy (token on top) and sell (token on the bottom) — same panel, no second screen.
Confirm in your wallet and the swap fires.
Slippage: how much price movement you'll accept on the fill. Fresh or volatile coins need more (try 5–10%). 100 bps = 1%.
9

Bundle

Buy or sell the same coin across several wallets at once — perfect for spreading a launch buy or exiting together with a planned ladder.

Paste the token CA and pick the wallets to include.
Choose a preset, or set staggered, varied amounts so the buys aren't all identical.
Add a laddered take-profit / stop-loss with a min and max — so it doesn't profit-take too low, and sells in steps instead of one big dump.
Fire the bundle. Buys and sells go out fast across every selected wallet.
The min/max ladder means your bundle takes some profit early but rides the rest toward your max target — natural, staged exits instead of one all-or-nothing clip.

Volume Bot

Generate lifelike trading volume on a token. You fund it from one wallet and it does the rest — automatically.

Set the token CA and fund it from your wallet — that's the only number you have to pick.
It creates ghost wallets in the background as needed — unlimited, no setup — and buys and sells from different wallets so the flow looks organic.
Tune the feel: min/max buy, speed (low / med / high) and a chart shape (steady, waves, organic, uptrend). Sells vary in size, and a partial sell is finished off later so nothing looks robotic.
Optionally leave a token in each wallet to bump the holder count. It runs until the funded SOL is spent, then sweeps everything back to your wallet.
Real money: the volume bot spends real SOL across many trades and fees add up. Start with a small fund and a short run to see how it behaves.

Copy / KOL Trade

Follow the wallets that know what they're doing. The Copy tab lists known KOLs with their picture and ROI, plus a custom box for any wallet.

Browse the KOL list — each shows a pfp, ROI and recent activity.
Tap a KOL to open their profile: current positions, social links and their track record.
Hit Copy to mirror their trades, or paste any wallet address to copy a wallet that isn't on the list.
Copy-trading isn't a guarantee — even great wallets take losses. Size your copy trades like any other bet.

Stay Safe

  • Only fund what you can lose. Memecoins are high-risk — your trading wallet should hold only your play money.
  • Check the security panel before you ape: holders, dev holds, LP burned, mint & freeze authority.
  • Save your wallet backup offline. It's the only way back in.
  • You always sign. SlimeWire builds the trade; the transfer that moves your SOL is approved in your own wallet. Never share your seed phrase or recovery file with anyone.
  • Tools spend real SOL. Bundle, Volume and Launch fire real trades and fees. Start small.
The loop: find a coin in Trending → check the security panel → 1-tap buy → take profit in Portfolio → repeat. 🐸🔥
