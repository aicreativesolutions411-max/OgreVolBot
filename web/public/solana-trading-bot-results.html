FAQ

What are Solana trading bot results?

Results can include tracked calls, alerts, realized PnL, position history, public receipts, and proof pages that show outcomes instead of promises.

Does proof guarantee future wins?

No. Proof can help users evaluate activity and history, but crypto trading remains risky and past results do not guarantee future outcomes.

How does SlimeWire use proof?
