Know what controls your money

How your funds work.

SlimeWire combines a profile, user-controlled trading wallets, and optional server-side automation. These are related, but they are not the same thing.

1

Create or log in

Your SlimeWire account stores your profile, wallet list, preferences, referrals, and automation settings so you can return on another device.

2

Choose a wallet

Connect Phantom/Solflare or use a user-controlled SlimeWire trading wallet. Every trade shows which wallet is paying.

3

Review and trade

Check the token, contract, network, size, slippage, fees, expected receive amount, and risk state before execution.

4

Arm automation

TP/SL, snipers, and copy rules run on SlimeWire servers after you explicitly configure them, so they can continue when your browser closes.

5

Back up recovery

Account recovery and wallet-key recovery are separate. Save every downloaded recovery file offline and never send it to support or a stranger.

6

Exit or disable

Cancel armed rules, withdraw funds, export keys where available, revoke connected sessions, and keep at least one main wallet attached to the profile.

What “user-controlled” means

Use the same wording everywhere so users know which recovery and approval model applies.

Connected wallet

Phantom or Solflare holds the key and presents the signing request. SlimeWire should not receive your seed phrase or private key.

SlimeWire trading wallet

The application creates or imports a dedicated wallet for faster trading and automation. Its encrypted record can be used by authorized server-side rules. The exported wallet key controls the funds and must be protected.

Account/profile

A username and password restore the SlimeWire account state; they are not a substitute for the separate wallet recovery files.

Automation permission

An armed rule authorizes the configured action within its wallet, size, slippage, and trigger settings. Disarming it stops future triggers; it does not reverse completed trades.

Recovery rule

Anyone with a raw wallet key can control that wallet. SlimeWire support will never need your seed phrase, private key, password, or recovery file in a DM.

Before money moves

  • Verify the full contract and network.
  • Confirm the paying wallet and maximum spend.
  • Review the current platform fee and live network/venue costs shown for that transaction.
  • Check slippage, price impact, minimum receive, and risk flags.
