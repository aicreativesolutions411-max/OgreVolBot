FAQ

What are SlimeWire widgets?

SlimeWire widgets are lightweight embeddable badges that route users to SlimeWire scans, charts, proof pages, raid pages, or @SlimeWiredBot.

Can launch teams use the widgets?

Yes. Launch teams can place the badges on token pages, campaign pages, link-in-bio pages, and group resource pages.

Do widgets trade from another website?

No. Widgets are links and iframe badges. Wallet actions still happen through SlimeWire or the user's wallet flow.

Do widgets help SEO?

They can help because they create useful, contextual SlimeWire links from community pages without needing spam or fake traffic.

Are the widgets heavy?

No. The script is tiny and creates a single iframe for each badge.
