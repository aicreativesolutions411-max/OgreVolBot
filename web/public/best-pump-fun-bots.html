FAQ

What should the best pump.fun bot include?

A useful pump.fun bot should show fresh launches, chart links, token context, Telegram alerts, quick trade handoff, presets, and visible risk notes.

Is a pump.fun bot the same as a scanner?

Not always. A scanner finds and filters launches, while a trading bot adds wallet, preset, alert, and execution workflows.

Can a pump.fun bot guarantee wins?

No. Fresh launches are risky. Good bots make context and exits faster, but they cannot guarantee profit or safety.
