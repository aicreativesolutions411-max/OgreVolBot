FAQ

What should Solana copy trading tools include?

They should include wallet tracking, token context, chart links, presets, risk notes, receipts, and PnL review.

Is copy trading always automatic?

No. Some workflows are advisory and some are automated. Users should understand permissions, sizing, and risks before following any wallet.

Can copy trading guarantee results?

No. Even successful wallets can enter bad trades, change behavior, or move too quickly for followers.
