What to look for

Fresh launch discovery

New coins need age, market cap, liquidity, and momentum context.

Safety context

Authority, holder concentration, metadata, links, and liquidity warnings matter before entry.

Chart handoff

Fast chart and transaction views help separate a real move from noise.

Telegram alerts

Mobile and group traders need scan cards and alerts where they already are.

Trade presets

Preset amount, slippage, TP/SL, timers, and exit size make fast trades less chaotic.

Position tracking

After entry, the tool should still show balance, position, and PnL context.
