Core lessons

What is a Solana Telegram bot?

A bot brings scans, alerts, chart links, and quick actions into Telegram so traders can react from mobile or group chats.

What is a trading terminal?

A terminal keeps live pairs, charts, transactions, wallet state, positions, PnL, and trade controls in one screen.

What is a rug checker?

A risk scanner surfaces visible liquidity, authority, concentration, metadata, and market signals before you buy.

What are TP/SL presets?

Take-profit, stop-loss, slippage, timer, and size presets help traders prepare a plan before a fast trade.

What is copy trading?

Copy tools watch selected wallets and help route activity through your own presets and wallets.

What are launch tools?

Launch tools help communities organize visibility, scans, alerts, charts, and trader handoff around new Solana coins.
