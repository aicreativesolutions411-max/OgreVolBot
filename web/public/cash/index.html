Trust & provider status

SlimeCash is a self-custodial interface for a user-controlled Solana wallet. It is not a bank account, and USDC is not cash or an FDIC-insured deposit.

Live
SOL and canonical Solana USDC sends, exact tracked requests, private receipts, encrypted wallet recovery, spend PINs, and daily limits.

Fee
SlimeCash sends add a 0.5% application fee. Solana network costs and possible token-account rent are separate.

Coinbase hosted
Coinbase controls verification, payment methods, regions, pricing, and limits. SlimeCash never asks for Coinbase credentials.

Approval required
Direct deposit, a SlimeCash card, and Apple Pay or Google Wallet are not live. They require a regulated partner, issuer/bank, user verification, disputes, and wallet-tokenization approval.

Never share a seed phrase, private key, recovery key, or spend PIN. Blockchain transfers are generally irreversible—verify the full recipient.

Install SlimeCash

SlimeCash installs as its own app with its own green dollar icon. It stays separate from SlimeWire on your home screen while securely sharing the same account and wallets.

Cash out

SlimeCash never touches your bank — you send dollars to an app you already use and cash out there.

Venmo / PayPal (PYUSD) — free 1:1 dollars
1. In Venmo or PayPal open Crypto → PYUSD → Receive and copy its address
2. Tap below and paste that address
3. Back in Venmo/PayPal: sell PYUSD (no fee) → transfer to bank
Coinbase — works with USDC or SOL
1. In Coinbase tap Receive, pick USDC on the SOLANA network, copy the address
2. Tap below and paste it
3. Sell in Coinbase → withdraw to your bank
Always double-check the receiving app shows the Solana network. Sends carry the standard 0.5% app fee.
