Before you approve

Confirm the paying wallet, network, token and full contract address, amount, slippage limit, estimated receive amount, and current risk flags.

Price impact and slippage can cost more than the platform fee on thin-liquidity tokens.

Fast mode should remain optional and visibly enabled when active.

Exact network, venue, launch-provider, and price-impact costs cannot be fixed in advance. If a confirmation screen and this page disagree, do not approve the transaction; refresh the quote and contact support. Promotional or token-specific reward programs must disclose any different total fee before execution.
