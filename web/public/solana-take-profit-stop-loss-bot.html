FAQ

What is a Solana TP/SL bot?

A Solana TP/SL bot helps users configure take-profit, stop-loss, and sometimes timer-based exits for trades.

Can TP/SL prevent every loss?

No. Liquidity, slippage, route failures, wallet state, and fast markets can affect exits.

Why pair TP/SL with PnL tracking?

PnL tracking helps users review whether their preset exits and trade plans actually worked over time.
