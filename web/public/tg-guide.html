Start here — bot & wallet

Member

Open @OgreTradeBot in Telegram and tap Start. Your wallet is created for you and lives inside the bot — non-custodial to you, no sign-up, no seed phrase to write down.

Open the bot. Search @OgreTradeBot → tap Start. A menu appears.
See your wallet. Tap Wallet (or send /wallet). It shows your SlimeWire address and SOL balance.
Fund it. Send SOL to that address from any wallet or exchange. Balance updates in seconds.
Save your backup. On creation the bot hands you a backup. Keep it — it's how you restore your wallet if you ever lose the session.
Everything trades from this wallet, your own funds. The bot only ever acts on a buy or sell you tap or pre-authorize — it never moves money on its own.
SlimeWire will never DM you first asking for a seed phrase or to "verify" your wallet on another site. That's always a scam.

Trade from your DMs

Member

Paste any coin's contract address (CA) or type $TICKER in your DM — the bot pulls a card with the price, safety read and a ⚡ Buy button. That's the whole loop.

⚡ 1-Click Buy (set it once)

Set a default amount + optional exits, and after that ⚡ Buy on any coin fires instantly with those settings.

  • Amount — how much SOL per tap (0.1, 0.5, 1, or custom).
  • Take-Profit / Stop-Loss — auto-sell at your targets (optional).
  • Trailing stop + break-even — lock gains as a winner runs; the trailing stop only arms once you're in profit, so it never sells you at a loss.

Manage & exit

📈 /positions

Your open bags, live value and PnL.

💰 /pnl

Your realized profit + a shareable slime PnL card.

🔴 Sell

Tap Sell on any position — 25/50/100% or a custom amount.

📊 /chart <CA>

A quick chart with timeframe buttons.

No exits set = you hold and manage it yourself. Turn on Exit Radar (Section 5) to get a take-profit ping when a bag tops.

Scan any coin

Member

Drop a CA or $TICKER in a group (where Scan is on) or in your DM and SlimeWire answers with a full scan card in seconds.

What the card shows

  • Stats — price, market cap, liquidity, volume, 1h buys/sells, age.
  • Security — top-10 holder concentration, holder count, dev-sold, mint & freeze authority (🟢 revoked / 🔴 active).
  • Socials — X, website, Telegram, Pump.
  • Who called it — the first caller here + how it's done since.

The buttons

⚡ Buy on SlimeWire

Opens the coin ready to buy.

📈 Chart

Live SlimeWire chart.

📊 Menu

Opens the deep-dive tools → Section 4.

🔄 Refresh

Re-pull the live read.

If Security shows n/a for a fresh coin, SlimeWire fills it straight from the chain — authorities are read off the mint account, so they're almost never blank.

The scan menu — the deep tools

Member

Tap 📊 Menu on any scan card for the tools that make SlimeWire different. Each opens right on the same card (no chat spam) with a Back button.

🕵️ Alpha Radar

Is a big network behind this coin? Counts proven-winner wallets, known operators and coordinated funding, and reads whether it has long-term-runner shape — not a fast pump.

🧠 AI Read

One honest 🟢/🟡/🔴 verdict + a 0–100 conviction score, built from SlimeShield, holders, dev-sold, authorities and live flow. No hype.

💸 Track the Funds

Follow the money — top-10 concentration, holder breakdown, sniper/insider share, dev-sold and live authorities. "Who can move the price?"

🔒 · 📈 · 🔗 · 🛠

Security, Charts, Socials and Trade-Tools link lists, each loaded with that exact coin.

All three reads are read-only — they never trade for you. Use them to decide, then tap Buy yourself.

Signals — Exit & Alpha Radar

Member

Send /signals in your DM for one menu of opt-in alerts. Toggle what you want — off by default, so nothing floods.

🎯 Exit Radar the sell signal nobody gives

Watches your own open bags and DMs you to take profit the moment one tops — a hard fade off the peak, or the dev dumping. It's advisory: it tells you, you decide. Everyone shills entries and ghosts you at the top; this is the opposite.

🕵️ Alpha Radar

Pings you when a coin a big network is quietly backing confirms a sustained climb — the hold-for-days runners, not fast pops. Fewer, higher-quality alerts.

In a group, an admin can turn Alpha Radar on for the whole channel (it replaces the frequent short-term plays with these rarer, better ones).

The Room — proof, not screenshots

Member

Send /room in the group. The Room turns a chat into a team with a verifiable board — the one thing no other bot can do, because we're in the chat and everyone has a wallet.

🏆 Room PnL

A live leaderboard of members' real realized SOL — from their own SlimeWire trades — and "the room is +X ◎ banked".

📢 Top callers

A call only counts if you actually held the coin (verified on-chain). Kills the "call everything, screenshot the winners" grift.

🧾 Proof-of-Call

A shareable receipt of your best verified winning call — provably yours, flex it.

✅ Join / 🚪 Leave

Fully opt-in. Join to compete on the board; leave any time.

To get on the board, tap ✅ Join board. Your realized SOL and verified calls only count once you opt in.

Copy the room's best

Member

Send /copy (or Trench → Copy the Best). Instead of copying anonymous whales, mirror a proven trader from your own Room board — someone you can see the real PnL of.

Pick a trader. The menu ranks the room's top members by real PnL — tap "Mirror".
Set your size. Choose how much SOL to mirror each of their buys (0.02–5).
You're live. When they buy through SlimeWire, your own wallet auto-buys the same coin at your size.
Stop any time. Tap 🚫 Stop copying.
Real money, your own wallet: you auto-buy whatever they ape. It's capped, non-custodial and off by default — start with a tiny size to feel it out.

Community Snipe — get in together

Member

When a dev sets up a Community Snipe, the whole room can get into their launch at the same second — each from their own wallet. No pooling, nobody holds your money.

Join. Send /snipe and tap an amount (💰 0.1 / 0.5 / 1, or Custom). That much auto-buys from your wallet the instant it launches.
Add presets (optional). Tap 🎯 TP/SL to set take-profit / stop-loss, or skip and manage it yourself.
Wait for launch. The moment the dev's wallet deploys (or they hit Fire), everyone's in — you get a DM confirmation.
Leave any time. Tap 🚪 Leave before it fires.
Sniping launches is high-risk — coins can rug instantly. Only your own funds, only what you set.

ADMIN — Rose & Shield

Admin

Open 🛡️ Rose & Shield from /menu. Full moderation + anti-scam, each a clean toggle.

🤖 Captcha / 🌐 Web verify

Mute new joins until they prove they're human (or connect a wallet).

🛡️ Anti-scam

Delete scam/drainer links, ghost "deleted accounts", impersonators, and a shared known-scammer ban list.

ADMIN — Trench setup

Admin

🎯 Community Snipe /snipe → ⚙️ Dev setup

  • 🎯 Set launch wallet — paste the wallet you'll launch from (it follows the wallet, so launch anywhere).
  • 🟢 Arm — the room fires the instant that wallet deploys. Fires once, then disarms.
  • 🔥 Fire NOW — the second you tweet the CA, paste it here and everyone snipes immediately (the reliable "tweet-to-snipe").
  • 🏆 Throne mode — everyone enters the same second with aggressive slippage so the fills land together.

🚀 Launch Room · 🕵️ Alpha Radar · 🎟️ Referral

  • Launch Room — a guided flow that ties Snipe + Raid + The Room + an "Announce launch" broadcast into one.
  • Alpha Radar (group) — /alpharadar on makes the group get the network-backed runner alerts instead of the frequent short-term plays.
  • Referral contest — open /settings → 🎟️ Referral Contest. Set a reward (💰 SOL or 🪙 a coin + amount) and 🏅 how many win (top 1 / 3 / 5 / 10 — more winners = more people racing), then Start. Members grab /reflink — it gives them a group invite (new joins count) and a site link (friends who make a wallet + trade on slimewire.org count too). /refboard shows the live leaderboard with each source (👥 joins · 🌐 site) and marks who's currently in the prizes (🏆). At the end the top N are announced with their payout wallets — you send each reward from your own Wallet → Send.
