Get Started

Open @SlimeWireBot in Telegram and press Start. That's it — no sign-up, no seed phrase.

Open the bot. Tap /start or /menu any time to bring up the main menu with every button.
Get a wallet. From the menu, create a SlimeWire wallet — your backup downloads instantly. Keep that backup safe; it's the only copy.
Fund it. Send a little SOL to your new wallet address (the bot shows it under /wallets). Start small.
Link the website (optional). Send /web for a one-time login code, then enter it at slimewire.org — same wallets, same positions, on the big screen.
Tip: Paste any Solana contract address (CA) straight into the chat and the bot instantly replies with a SlimeShield verdict, chart, and trade buttons — no command needed.

Trading — in your DMs

Trading runs in a private chat with the bot (it'll say "open me in DM to trade" if you try it in a group — your wallet stays private). Here's the full command set:

/trade
Open the buy / sell menu — pick a token, amount, and wallet, then confirm.
/buy
Jump straight to a buy. Paste the CA when asked (or send /buy with a CA), set your SOL amount, confirm.
/sell
Sell a position — choose the coin and the % to sell (25 / 50 / 100 or custom).
paste a CA
Drops a scan card with one-tap Quick Buy + chart. The fastest way in.
/positions
Your open bags — live price, P&L %, and sell buttons on each.
/pnl
Your results + a shareable PnL card. Honest accounting (near-breakevens count as scratches, not fake wins).
/wallets
Your saved wallets + addresses. Create, back up, or switch the active one.
/balances
SOL + token balances across your wallets.

How a buy works

Pick the coin. Paste a CA, or tap a coin from a scan / /alpha card.
Set the amount. Enter how much SOL to spend. Slippage and your active wallet are shown — adjust if needed.
Confirm. The bot fires the swap and replies with the fill. The coin appears under /positions.
Sell when ready. Open /positions and tap a sell %, or send /sell. Exits use escalating slippage so the order actually lands on a fast-moving curve.
Real money. Every buy/sell is a live on-chain trade. The bot reads risk (SlimeShield) and shows warnings, but it never guarantees profit and won't override a clear danger signal silently. Trade amounts you can afford to lose.

Scan Bot — read any coin

Scan Bot is your instant due-diligence. It works in your DMs and in any group where an admin turned it on. Drop a CA, get the truth.

/look <CA>
Full SlimeShield read on a token — verdict (BUY / CAUTION / RISK / AVOID), liquidity, MC, age, dev/security flags, socials, chart + trade buttons.
paste a CA
Same scan card, no command. In groups this is the Scan Bot auto-reply (admins toggle it).
/alpha
The top scanner picks right now — the best-scoring live coins.
/ape
One fresh low-MC shot right now, for when you just want the next degen play.
/kols
What tracked KOL / smart-money wallets are buying this minute.
/receipts
SlimeShield's rug-call receipts + hit rate — proof the shield actually catches them.
/proof
The live engine track record + proof wall.
Group bonus: with Scan Bot on, pasting an X / tweet link in the group unfurls the full post (image/video + text) right in chat.
SlimeShield verdicts: BUY = clean read · CAUTION = trade small / use protection · RISK = watch only · AVOID = genuine danger (active mint/freeze authority, honeypot signals). Brand-new coins start "checking" until the on-chain read lands.

Raid Bot — rally your crew on X

Raid Bot turns an X post into a live, tracked engagement raid for your group: set the targets, it watches likes / reposts / replies / bookmarks and reports progress, with a public board.

/raid <X post link>
Opens the raid menu — pick targets (likes, reposts, replies, bookmarks), timer, and reward, then launch.
/raid <link> $SYM 500 150 80 50
Power form — skip the menu. That's 500 likes · 150 reposts · 80 replies · 50 bookmarks for $SYM.
Drop the post. Send /raid with your X post link in the group.
Set the goal. Use the menu, or the power form above to set it in one line.
Rally. The bot pins the targets and refreshes engagement, ticking each metric toward its goal.
Track it anywhere. Every raid also shows on the board → slimewire.org/raids
Engagement is read for free (no paid X API key needed), so raids just work out of the box.

Buy Bot — live buy alerts in your group

Buy Bot posts a card in your group every time someone buys your coin — the classic "green candle hype" loop, with a one-tap Quick-Buy link so newcomers can ape right back.

Turn it on. In the group, an admin sends /buybot on (or opens /settings).
Lock the token. Paste your coin's CA in the group — Buy Bot locks onto it and starts posting its buys. (Change it any time by setting a new token.)
Tune the alerts. Use the commands below to hide tiny buys and set your hype emoji.
/buybot on · off
Turn Buy Bot on or off for this group (admins).
/minbuy 0.5
Only alert on buys ≥ 0.5 SOL (0 = show every buy). Cuts dust spam.
/setbuyemoji 🐸 0.1
Set the hype emoji + how much SOL each emoji = . Here: one 🐸 per 0.1 SOL, so a 1 SOL buy shows ten 🐸.
Default emoji: 🟢 at one per 0.15 SOL. Pick anything that matches your coin's vibe — bigger buys = a longer emoji wall = more hype.

Group Setup & Toggles

Add @SlimeWireBot to your group, make it an admin (so it can post + read), then turn on the modules you want. Every module is off by default and only an admin can flip them.

/settings
The control panel — see which modules are on and toggle them with buttons.
/scan on · off
Scan Bot — auto-scan pasted CAs + unfurl X links.
/buybot on · off
Buy Bot — live buy-alert cards (see section 5).
/raid on · off
Raid Bot — the /raid command + engagement tracking (see section 4).
/rose on · off
Rose Manager — new-member welcome, anti-link filtering, and moderation.
/slimewire on · off
SlimeWire engine alerts in this chat (admins) — the bot's own live signals.
/mywins on · off
Post your take-profit wins in this chat — your own flex, opt-in per chat.
Admins only. Module toggles are admin-gated, and the bot needs admin rights in the group to post alerts and read messages. Add it as an admin first or the toggles won't take.
