FAQ

What is a Solana rug checker?

A Solana rug checker reviews visible token and market signals such as liquidity, holder concentration, authority risks, sellability warnings, and transaction behavior before you trade.

What does SlimeShield scan?

SlimeShield looks at available token data, liquidity, age, buy and sell flow, holder concentration, authority risk notes, third-party warnings, and SlimeWire's own cached market context.

Can a rug checker guarantee a token is safe?

No. A scanner can reduce blind risk, but no tool can guarantee a fresh memecoin will not rug later. SlimeWire shows the evidence and recommends safer sizing or avoiding risky setups.

Can I scan from Telegram?

Yes. Paste a contract address or ticker into @SlimeWiredBot to get a SlimeShield-style read and links back to the web terminal.

What should I do after a risky scan?

If SlimeWire marks a token as RISK or AVOID, consider watching only, using protected buys with smaller size, or skipping the trade entirely.
