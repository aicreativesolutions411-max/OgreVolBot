FAQ

Does SlimeWire need my seed phrase?

No. Never share a seed phrase or recovery phrase with SlimeWire, Telegram, support accounts, or any website.

Can token scans prove safety?

No. Token scans can surface risk context, but they cannot guarantee a token is safe.

How should users avoid Telegram impersonators?

Users should verify the official bot handle, avoid unsolicited DMs, and use links from the official site.
