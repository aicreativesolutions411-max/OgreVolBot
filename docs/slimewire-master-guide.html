1. The big picture

SlimeWire is three layers that feed each other:

  • The terminal (slimewire.org) — live pairs, charts, one-tap trading, risk reads, and managed wallets that can run automation while your browser is closed.
  • The engine (server) — TP/SL watchers, the scanner, SlimeShield scoring, Ogre A.I. autopilot, KOL copy plans, and the alpha-call tracker. It runs 24/7.
  • The megaphone (Telegram bot + Proof Wall) — the engine's activity becomes public posts: plays every 15 minutes, win receipts, rug-flag receipts. Every post links back into the terminal with the buy panel ready.

The flywheel: drops create attention → receipts create trust → deep links create users → users create more engine activity.

2. The terminal: feeds, quick buy, SlimeShield

Feeds

  • Live Terminal / Cooks — the main live feed. Rows refresh in place (your scroll position never jumps).
  • Live Pairs — bucketed fresh pairs (live / under 1h / 3h / 1d) with best/newest sorting.
  • Slime Scope — the scanner view with deeper per-row stats.
  • Watchlist — tap Watch on any row to save a coin; Saved rows show a star.

Quick Buy

Set your amount once in the Quick Buy SOL box on any feed toolbar (or pick a preset). Every Buy button across the whole site instantly reads "Buy 0.1 SOL" with your live amount and fires with one tap. You can also just tell the chat agent: "set quick buy to 0.5".

SlimeShield

Every pair gets a verdict: BUY / CAUTION / RISK / AVOID with a 0–100 score, built from liquidity, age, volume flow, sell pressure, bundle risk, mint/freeze authority, dev-wallet history, and KOL dump pressure. The shield isn't advisory decoration — it actively gates Ogre A.I. buys and feeds the public receipts system.

Maximize it: keep a preset with your default TP/SL and size, then trade entirely from feed rows: see pair → one tap Buy → exits armed automatically. The terminal is built so you never need the trade desk for routine entries.

3. Trading & server automation

Wallets: two kinds, different powers

Wallet typeWhat it can do
Connected (Phantom / Solflare / Backpack)Buy/sell with a wallet-approval popup each time. View-only positions tracking.
Managed (SlimeWire wallets)Everything automated: server TP/SL, timers, Bundle, SlimeBot, Sniper, Ogre A.I., KOL copy — runs with your browser closed. Backup files download on creation; keep them.

Server TP/SL (the core safety net)

  • Arm take-profit / stop-loss / timer exits on any buy. The server watches prices every ~2 seconds and executes without you.
  • Requires the TP/SL automation permission (the "Enable TP/SL" button in the top bar). It expires periodically — the site now shows a loud red warning when armed plans are running on an expiring/expired permission. Re-enable immediately when you see it.
  • Track every armed plan on the TP/SL Audit page (Tx Audit tab): status, per-wallet triggers, last price checks.

Bundle

One token bought/sold across many wallets at once, with auto-exit settings. Fast Bundle presets let you bundle-buy straight from a feed row (with an in-app confirm showing the wallet count).

SlimeBot (Volume)

The auto-volume engine: deploys a SOL budget over a duration as randomized buys/sells across recycled wallets (smart or spam mode, aggressiveness levels), then sweeps everything back to your source wallet. Confirm dialog shows the full deployment math before it spends.

OgreSniper & Launch Watch

Sniper scans seven modes (Safe / Smart / Fast / PumpSnipe / Low-MC / Narratives / Long) and one-tap snipes with your per-wallet amount and exits. Launch Watch arms a watcher for a ticker so the buy fires the moment it goes live.

4. Ogre A.I. & Autopilot

  • Manual runs: pick a category, amount, and exits — Ogre A.I. scans, ranks, and apes the best pick into a managed plan with TP/SL armed.
  • Autopilot: fully unattended. Hard guards you set: max SOL per buy, max spend per hour, max concurrent plans, minimum score, interval. Default OFF; you arm it.
  • The shield gate: every Ogre A.I. buy runs a final SlimeShield check at purchase time. Only AVOID blocks — risky setups are allowed on purpose (they're often the biggest winners); your TP/SL plan is the risk control.
  • It avoids repeating recent mints and diversifies across picks.
Maximize it: run autopilot small and steady (e.g. 0.05 SOL per buy, 0.3 SOL/hour cap, 2 concurrent) with a -8% stop. The math of many small shielded entries with hard stops is the win-rate play; the engine record on the Proof Wall will show it working.

5. KOL Tracker & copy-trading

  • The KOL page tracks known trader wallets: live signals, positions, leaderboard.
  • Copy Wallet Next Buy: pick a tracked wallet, set amount + exits — SlimeWire mirrors their next buy from your wallets within one ~30s scan tick and arms TP/SL on the copied bag automatically.
  • KOL Dump Detector flags wallets that tend to sell into their followers — check it before copying anyone.

6. Pump Launch + Jito anti-snipe

  • Create a pump.fun token from the Pump Launch page: coin details, image, socials, fees, dev buy, optional bundle buys.
  • Image pipeline is verified: uploads go to pump.fun's own IPFS first, and the server confirms the image actually loads before the coin is created. Launching with no image now asks explicitly before using the default logo.
  • Jito anti-snipe is ON: create + dev buy + first-block bundle buys land atomically in one Jito bundle — snipers physically cannot get between your create and your buys. Do your first launch with a small dev buy and watch it.
  • Successful launches auto-post to your opted-in Telegram groups/channel with pump.fun + chart links — free promotion for every launch through your launcher.

7. Positions, PnL & share cards

  • Each position row shows: live value, entry cost, current MC, open PnL, TP/SL armed state (or "no auto-exit armed"), and a green/red "▲ +0.012 SOL since last refresh" change note.
  • Arm Exits button opens the trade ticket preloaded for that bag. Clicking the row opens its chart — everything stays inside SlimeWire.
  • Sell 25/50/100% or custom % directly from the row (custom uses the in-app dialog).
  • PnL cards: on the PnL page, Card downloads a branded PNG of the trade, Post shares it. In the Telegram DM, /pnl <token> or /card <token> sends the same card.

8. Push alerts (phone notifications)

  • Enable: Profile → Alerts → "Enable Push Alerts". Up to 5 devices per account.
  • iPhone: first add SlimeWire to your Home Screen (Share → Add to Home Screen), open it from there, then enable.
  • You get pinged when: a take-profit or stop-loss fires, a plan fails, or a KOL copy executes — with the site completely closed. Tapping the notification opens the TP/SL audit.
Maximize it: push alerts + server TP/SL = walk away from the screen. Arm the plan, close the laptop, get pinged when money moves. This is the feature that makes armed plans trustworthy.

Fast Mode lets the agent execute trades from chat without an extra tap — you approve the auto-trade session first, and you can revoke any time.

What an armed chat receives

  • Plays every 15 minutes: 3–4 picks, each labeled 🟢 BUY / 🟡 CAUTION / 🟠 RISK with its shield score (AVOID never posts), with the honest engine record in the footer ("Engine record: 12W-9L").
  • Win receipts: when a dropped pick 2x's, the chat gets "✅ Called it: $XYZ is 2.4x since the drop 3h ago — First dropped in [your group]".
  • Engine events: fresh SlimeWire launches, TP/SL fires ("stop-loss saved a bag"), KOL copy executions — all anonymous, never wallet info.
  • Hard anti-spam: max 8 posts/hour per chat, minimum 2 minutes apart, dedupe — enforced in code, drops instead of queueing.

  • Chart deep links paint an instant loading skeleton, so taps feel immediate.

11. The Proof Wall & growth loops

slimewire.org/proof — public, no login, loads instantly. Shows: calls tracked, 2x wins with time-to-win, hit rate, best call, rugs SlimeShield flagged first (with minutes-of-warning), live-tracking calls, and the Group Alpha League (top groups ranked by wins their drops produced — groups compete to host the bot).

Everything links here: drops, win receipts, /proof, /receipts, bot keyboards. The page is the answer to "is this legit" — and it includes the losses, which is exactly why it works.

Your playbook: (1) add the bot to every group you're in, get admins to /slimewire on; (2) screenshot win receipts to X with the /proof link; (3) launch coins through Pump Launch so every launch self-promotes; (4) share PnL cards after wins. Every piece feeds the same loop.

Costs & housekeeping

  • Render build minutes: every git push builds 3 services. Set Auto-Deploy: No on the two background workers (they only call the web service over HTTP) to cut the burn by two-thirds. Top up minutes before big shipping days.
  • All A.I. is free-tier (Groq/Gemini/OpenRouter). If the agent ever degrades, check those keys' quotas first.
  • Backups: managed-wallet backup files download on creation and removal — store them; they're the only restore path.
