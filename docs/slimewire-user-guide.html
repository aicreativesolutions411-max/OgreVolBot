1. Getting started

  1. Go to slimewire.org. After the intro, hit Connect — Phantom, Solflare, or Backpack — or create an account with a username/email.
  2. You land on the Live Terminal: fresh pairs cooking right now.
  3. Two wallet types matter:
    • Connected wallet (Phantom etc.) — you approve each trade in the wallet popup.
    • Managed wallets (created in Wallets) — unlock everything automatic: server stop-losses that fire while you sleep, snipers, bots, A.I. buying. Backup files download when you create one — save them; they are the only restore path.
  4. Tap Enable TP/SL in the top bar once — that permission lets the server execute your exits. If it ever expires with plans armed, a red warning appears: re-enable immediately.

2. Finding your way around

  • Desktop: the top bar has six areas with drop-down menus — Live (terminal, cooks, trades), Chart & Swap, Intel (scope, watchlist, KOL, sniper, TP/SL audit), Ogre Tek (A.I., launch, bundle, SlimeBot, launch watch), Portfolio (wallets, positions, PnL), Profile. Everything is two clicks away.
  • Mobile: the right-side rail holds the same tools — scroll it if your screen is short.
  • Fastest way anywhere: open the Ask bubble and type where you want to go — "open my bags", "where is the sniper", "take me to pnl". It navigates instantly.

3. Reading pairs & SlimeShield

Every pair row shows MC, liquidity, age, volume, and a SlimeShield verdict:

VerdictMeaning
🟢 BUYCleaner setup: real liquidity, healthy flow, no hard flags.
🟡 CAUTIONTradeable but watch it — something is mid.
🟠 RISKDegen territory. Can run hard; size like it can go to zero.
🔴 AVOIDHard flags (mint/freeze risk, honeypot pattern, dev history). The site blocks quick-buys on the worst of these.

The score (0–100) folds in liquidity, age, volume flow, sell pressure, bundle risk, dev wallet history, and KOL dump pressure. Check the shield's public track record any time at slimewire.org/proof.

Tap any pair → its chart opens with transactions and the buy panel. Watch saves it to your Watchlist.

4. Buying & selling

Quick Buy — the one-tap entry

  1. Set your amount once in the Quick Buy SOL box on any feed (or pick a preset). Every Buy button site-wide now reads "Buy 0.1 SOL" with your amount.
  2. Tap Buy on any row. Done. Your preset's TP/SL arms automatically on managed wallets.
  3. Or tell the Ask bubble: "set quick buy to 0.5" — or even "buy 0.1 of <CA> with 25 tp 8 sl".

Selling

From Positions: Sell 25% / 50% / Exit 100% / Custom %. From the chart page: the trade panel sells with live route preview. Protected Buy presets add a guarded entry with partial take-profits pre-armed.

The golden setup: managed wallet + preset with TP/SL + quick buy amount set = see pair, one tap, protected. Everything else is optional.

5. Positions & PnL

  • Each position row answers "am I winning and am I protected": live value, entry cost, current MC, open PnL, TP/SL armed state, and a green/red "▲ +0.012 SOL since last refresh" note.
  • Arm Exits opens the trade ticket for that bag if nothing is protecting it.
  • PnL page: every closed trade with totals. Card downloads a branded PnL image; Post shares it to X (your referral link rides along automatically).

7. Launching a coin

  1. Ogre Tek → Pump Launch: name, ticker, image (the site checks your image actually reaches pump.fun before creating), socials, dev buy.
  2. Anti-snipe is on: your create + dev buy + bundle buys land in one atomic block — snipers cannot get between them.
  3. Your launch auto-posts to SlimeWire's Telegram channels and groups with pump.fun + chart links — free promotion.

8. The Call Board (token rooms)

Scroll under any chart: that token's board. Post a structured call — 🟢 Bullish / 🔴 Bearish / ⚠️ Warning / ❓ Question, optional 2x/5x/10x target, 140-character why. The site stamps your call with the shield score and entry MC, then tracks it automatically: hit 2x and your call shows "✅ hit 2.4x" forever. Your handle carries your record (wins + hit rate) next to every post — reputation comes from results. One call per token per 6 hours. Every call row has Buy and Watch buttons so good calls turn into action instantly.

9. Phone alerts

  1. Profile → Alerts → Enable Push Alerts.
  2. iPhone: add SlimeWire to your Home Screen first (Share → Add to Home Screen), open from there, then enable.
  3. You get pinged when a TP or SL fires, a plan fails, or a KOL copy executes — site closed, phone in pocket.

11. Sharing & X

  • The token page: slimewire.org/t?ca=<CA> — shield verdict, live stats, chart, and the call board, no login needed. This is the link to drop on X instead of Dex. "Saw it on X? Verify it on SlimeWire."
  • Signal cards: the 📸 button on any call board renders a branded share image (verdict, MC, stats, CA). Post it with your token page link.
  • PnL cards: from the PnL page after a win — the flex that brings people in.
  • The Proof Wall: slimewire.org/proof — the engine's full public record: 2x wins with time-to-win, rugs flagged with minutes-of-warning, top groups, live-tracked calls. Wins AND losses. Quote it whenever someone asks "is this legit".
  • Referral on every share: logged in, every X share you make automatically carries your referral link. Set your custom code in Profile → Referral (edit the part after /r/), add a payout wallet, and every flex earns.
The loop that grows your bags and the swamp: see a CA on X → verify on /t?ca= → call it on the board → buy with exits armed → TP fires → your win posts in your group with your name → share the card with your referral. Every step feeds the next.
